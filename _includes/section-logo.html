The Logo

We think that the logo, while it is effective in communicating a name, is strange in its presentation. The sans-serif font looks strange from a distance and the italics make it seem like something is off.

  • The italics are strange and off-putting
  • The color palette provides a weird contrast between desaturated blues and saturated golden yellow.
  • The visuals are very corporate and are focues away from a student audience and towards an investor audience.
