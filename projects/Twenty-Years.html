--- layout: project-large title: Twenty Years Magazine description: This massive multi-media project was created as part of a collaborative effort between Western Kentucky University and the College Heights Herald. The project involves a 48-page magazine and a website that explains the history behind WKU President Gary Ransdell's presidency and the impacts that he made on shaping the university into what it has become today.
I was on a design team of three other designers who were also part of a larger publication. I was in charge of the front half of the magazine specifically pages 7 to page 12.
The website concept was made by me but the actual design was made by Craig Ostertag and Nicole Ares. It uses Wordpress CMS and has three pages in total.
